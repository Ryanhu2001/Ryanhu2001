# LLM Era Personal Knowledge Base

## One Sentence

In the LLM era, a personal knowledge base should preserve judgment, context, evidence, and reusable thinking, not merely collected information.

## Source

Conversation notes and ongoing practice.

## My Judgment

LLMs make collection and summarization cheap. That makes selection more important. The scarce thing is not text; it is a traceable record of what I believe, why I believe it, and how it should change when new evidence appears.
